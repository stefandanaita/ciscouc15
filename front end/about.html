About ABC Design!


ABC Design or A Black Cat Design was founded in 2014 by one of the most talented graphic and web designers, Andreea Bertea.


She is the CEO of ABC Design and the company's name was inspired by her real name Andreea Bertea Camelia and a true passion for black cats and cats in general.


ABC Design has a very powerful mission and vission, we want to provide our customers with what they need for the lowest price available on the market. Dare to ask and we will work day and night so that your wish will come true
